💧 How To Play 💧

  • Select a pipe piece and click a grid cell to place it.
  • Each pipe gives +10 points.
  • Collect ⭐ stars for +100 points.
  • Click the 🐀 mouse before it escapes for +20 points.
  • If the mouse escapes you lose -10 points.
  • The mouse may destroy pipes.
  • Get the pipeline to the house to win the game!
